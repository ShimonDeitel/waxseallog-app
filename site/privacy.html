Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Offline-first, no data collection

Wax Seal Log is an offline-first app. All of your data (items, notes, and settings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. This app makes no network requests and collects no personal data of any kind. We do not operate any servers that receive your data, we do not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not share any information with third parties.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.
